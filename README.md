### Undergraduate Research Repo 

I do work in UC Berkeley's Moving Universe Lab (muLab) using data from the
James Webb Space Telescope. A first-author paper on my work deriving the
extinctino law of the Galactic Center is being drafted -- you can see its
progress [here](https://dev-undergrad.dev/book/draft.pdf).

I also work at Berkeley Lab's Advanced Quatum Testbed \& Quantum Nanoelectronics
Lab. I write software to design and simulate SQUID fluxonium architectures.
